Greetings, delegates!

Ten years ago, our alumni sought to give form to a dream; to provide a forum where students would not only polish their speaking skills but also gain insight into the policies and cultures of different countries. Their hard work made this dream a reality into the form of MUNIC, now an annual tradition at City School Capital Campus.

As the Secretary General of MUNIC TEN, it is my solemn duty to create an atmosphere of debates in which veterans may test their mettle with fellow peers and beginners can learn. However, that is not all I hope to achieve. This MUNIC, we are integrating technology with debates to make this MUN not only more interactive for the delegates but also push the diplomacy out of the committee room. Moreover, with the game changing app in development, I assure you that this MUNIC will be much closer to what real-life policymakers and diplomats go through when faced with crisis.

So I invite you all to MUNIC TEN, to debate passionately, to make friends, to make memories, to make history.

Muhammad Nafees

Secretary General

Since its conception in 2008, Model United Nations In City (MUNIC) has defined the aesthetics of debates and received numerous approbations from all it's constituents to date. The host team is working like never before to strengthen the conference's core structure, and we can not wait to be acquainted with you all

The history of MUNIC goes back ten years, it was a mere dream at the time. A dream, dreamt by the alumni of this esteemed instituition. A dream to allow pupils to not only speak their minds but to open it to the vast heritage of numerous countries around the globe. Through a lot of hardwork, put together by the alumni, MUNIC is now an annual tradition at City School.

As the President of MUNIC, it is my responsibility to create such an aura where practiced debaters can show their true mettle, which sets an example for fellow beginners and gives them a heads-up for further challenges.

Furthermore, this MUN is not only constricted to this. This MUNIC, we are integrating technology with debates to make this MUN not only more interactive for the delegates but also also to extract every bit of diplomacy each delegate has to offer. Moreover with the game changing app in development, one thing stays assured that this MUN will be a lot closer to what professional diplomats and negotiators go through when met with a major crisis.

Hence, I invite you all to get not only experience, but make memories, friends and get to know the MUNIC family simply by joining us at MUNIC TEN.

Muhammad Shujee

President
